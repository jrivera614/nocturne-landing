How It Works

Three steps. Five minutes.

We install a small sensor, it counts devices passively, and you get a live dashboard. That's the whole product.

1

We install a small sensor

A device the size of a deck of cards goes behind your bar or near the entrance. Plugs into a power outlet. Takes 5 minutes and we handle everything.

2

It counts devices passively

The sensor detects WiFi signals from phones nearby. No personal data is collected — just an anonymous device count. Your customers will never know it's there.

3

You get a live dashboard

Open your dashboard on any phone or laptop. Real-time crowd count, capacity percentage, tonight's trend chart, and push alerts when you're approaching capacity.
